Habits & Things

A minimal, beautiful app for tracking habits and things.
One screen, zero friction.

Habits & Things
Track recurring habits with flexible frequencies and one-off things with due dates. Switch between them instantly.
Counters & Streaks
Set a target like "50 pushups" and tap to increment. See your progress at a glance with simple counters.
Widgets
Small, medium, and lock screen widgets show your habit progress right on your home screen.
Gentle Reminders
Optional local notifications at the time you choose, on the schedule your habit follows.
Backup & Sync
Local JSON export and import. Optional cloud sync with your account across devices.
Beautiful Palettes
Four artist-inspired color themes. Follows your system dark and light mode automatically.
